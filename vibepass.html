Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about VibePass.
After your 30-day trial, your VibePass membership automatically converts to a paid subscription at the plan you selected (Monthly ₹49/month or Annual ₹299/year). We'll send you a reminder email 3 days before your trial ends. You can cancel at any time before the trial ends and you won't be charged anything.
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your VibePass membership at any time from your Account settings. If you cancel an annual plan, your benefits will continue until the end of the billing period — no partial refunds, but you keep everything you paid for. Monthly plans can be cancelled before the next billing cycle with no charges.
Free delivery applies to all orders fulfilled by VibeStore directly (Fulfilled by VibeStore label). For orders shipped by individual marketplace sellers, free delivery applies to sellers enrolled in the VibePass Delivery Program — look for the ⚡ VibePass badge on product pages. Exclusive deals apply to all VibePass-tagged products across all sellers.
VibeStream and VibeMusic are included in your VibePass subscription at no extra cost. Once your VibePass is active, you can access both services directly from your account. VibeStream offers movies, web series, and originals. VibeMusic gives you ad-free access to 60M+ tracks with offline downloads on up to 5 devices.
VibePass Student gives you all the same benefits as the Annual plan at 50% off (₹149/year instead of ₹299). To verify, you need a valid .edu email address from a recognised university or college. After signing up, we'll send a verification link to your .edu email. Once confirmed, your student pricing is locked in for up to 4 years or as long as you maintain a valid .edu email.
