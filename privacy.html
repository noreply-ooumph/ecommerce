Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our Platform. Here is a detailed breakdown of what we collect and why:

CategoryData CollectedPurpose
Account Information Full name, email address, phone number, delivery address(es), date of birth, profile photo Account creation, identity verification, communication
Transaction Data Order history, items purchased, payment methods used (tokenised), return and refund history, invoices Order processing, customer support, legal compliance
Behavioural Data Pages visited, products viewed, search queries, time spent on pages, items added to cart/wishlist, click patterns Personalisation, product recommendations, platform improvement
Device Data IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type (mobile/desktop/tablet), screen resolution Security, fraud detection, platform optimisation
Location Data City-level location (derived from IP or granted by user), pincode for delivery Delivery feasibility, regional pricing, logistics

What We Do NOT Collect: We do not store your full card numbers or bank account details. Payment information is processed and tokenised by our payment partner (Razorpay) and governed by their security standards.

2

How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing and Fulfilment: To process your orders, payments, and coordinate delivery with logistics partners. This is the primary purpose for which most data is collected.
  • Account Management: To create and maintain your account, verify your identity, and provide account-related services.
  • Personalisation: To personalise your shopping experience, including product recommendations, personalised deals, and curated content based on your browsing and purchase history.
  • Communications:
    • Transactional communications: Order confirmations, shipping updates, delivery alerts (always sent).
    • Promotional communications: Deals, new arrivals, special offers — sent only with your consent and can be opted out at any time.
    • Service notifications: Policy changes, security alerts, account notices.
  • Fraud Detection and Security: To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions, unauthorised access, and other illegal activities using behavioural and device data.
  • Platform Analytics: To analyse aggregate, anonymised usage patterns to improve our Platform's features, performance, and user experience.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable Indian laws, respond to lawful requests from government or regulatory authorities, and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your queries, complaints, and requests through our support channels.
3

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Sellers: When you place an order, we share only the order details necessary for the seller to fulfil your purchase (item details, delivery name and address, order ID). Your email, phone number, and full payment information are not shared with sellers.
  • With Payment Processors — Razorpay: We share payment-related information with Razorpay Financial Solutions Pvt Ltd to process transactions. Razorpay is PCI DSS compliant and processes data under their own privacy policy.
  • With Logistics Partners — Shiprocket: We share shipping details (recipient name, delivery address, order ID, item category) with Shiprocket and connected courier partners to facilitate delivery.
  • With Analytics Providers: We share only anonymised, aggregated data with analytics tools. No personally identifiable information is shared for analytics purposes.
  • With Legal and Regulatory Authorities: We may disclose your information when required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or when we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

We Never Sell Your Data: VibeStore will never sell, lease, or rent your personal information to any third party for commercial or marketing purposes. This is a firm commitment.

4

Cookies and Tracking

VibeStore uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on the Platform. Here is how we use them:

  • Essential / Session Cookies: These are strictly necessary cookies that enable core Platform functionality such as maintaining your shopping cart, login sessions, and preferences. These cannot be disabled without affecting Platform functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use first-party analytics cookies to understand how users navigate our Platform, which pages are most visited, and where users drop off. These cookies collect anonymised data only. You can disable analytics cookies via our Cookie Preferences centre accessible from the footer.
  • Preference Cookies: These remember your settings and preferences such as language, location, and display preferences to enhance your experience on return visits.

No Third-Party Advertising Cookies: VibeStore does not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We do not participate in cross-site tracking or retargeting advertising networks. Your browsing activity on VibeStore is not shared with advertisers.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via your browser settings or through our Cookie Preferences link in the footer. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impair Platform functionality.

Your Rights

In alignment with the Personal Data Protection Bill, 2023 and existing data protection principles under Indian law, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

Your Data Rights

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. We will provide this within 30 days of a verified request.
  • Right to Correction: You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you. You can also update most information directly from your Account Settings page.
  • Right to Deletion: You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated personal data. We will comply within 30 days, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on your consent (e.g., promotional communications), you may withdraw consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and to transmit it to another controller.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: You have the right to approach VibeStore's Grievance Officer with complaints regarding the processing of your personal data.

To exercise any of the above rights, please contact us at privacy@vibestore.in. We will respond to all verified requests within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by applicable law:

7

Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect it:

  • Infrastructure Security: Our Platform is hosted on Google Cloud / Firebase infrastructure, which provides enterprise-grade physical, environmental, and technical security controls. Google Cloud maintains SOC 2, ISO 27001, and PCI DSS certifications.
  • Data Transmission: All data transmitted between your device and VibeStore servers is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS 1.2 or higher). We enforce HTTPS across all Platform endpoints.
  • Password Security: Passwords are never stored in plain text. We use industry-standard hashing algorithms (bcrypt) with salting to protect password data.
  • Two-Factor Authentication: We offer two-factor authentication (2FA) for all user accounts. We strongly recommend enabling 2FA in your Account Security settings.
  • Access Controls: Access to user data within our organisation is restricted to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis, enforced through role-based access controls.
  • Security Audits: We conduct regular security assessments and penetration testing to identify and remediate vulnerabilities.

Security Incident Notification: In the event of a data breach that is likely to cause harm to affected individuals, we will notify impacted users and the relevant regulatory authority as required under applicable Indian law, within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

8

Children's Privacy

The VibeStore Platform is not directed to or intended for use by individuals under the age of 18 years. We do not knowingly collect, process, or store personal information from minors.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under the age of 18 without verifiable parental consent, we will take immediate steps to delete such information from our systems. If you believe we may have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately at privacy@vibestore.in.

Parents and guardians who wish to allow minors to use the Platform must do so under their active supervision and by consenting to these Terms on the minor's behalf.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Material Changes: For changes that materially affect how we collect, use, or share your personal information, we will provide at least thirty (30) days' advance notice via email to your registered email address and by posting a prominent notice on the Platform.

Your continued use of the Platform after the effective date of any updated Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. If you do not agree to the updated policy, please discontinue your use of the Platform and request account deletion.
