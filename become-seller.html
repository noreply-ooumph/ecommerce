Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about selling on VibeStore

How much does it cost to sell on VibeStore? +
The Individual plan is completely free — you only pay ₹20 per item sold. The Professional plan is ₹999/month with no per-item fees, unlimited listings, and advanced tools. New sellers on any plan enjoy 0% commission for the first 3 months.
What documents do I need to register as a seller? +
You need: PAN card, Aadhaar card (for individual/proprietorship), GST registration (optional but recommended for higher limits), bank account details with cancelled cheque, and a mobile number linked to your bank. For companies/LLPs, you'll also need incorporation certificate and MOA/AOA.
How and when do I get paid? +
Individual plan sellers receive payments within 7 business days of order delivery. Professional plan sellers get paid within 2 business days. Payments are made directly to your registered bank account via NEFT/IMPS. You can view all pending and processed payments in your Seller Central dashboard.
What is VibeStore Fulfilled (FBV)? +
VibeStore Fulfilled (FBV) means we store your products in our fulfillment centers across India. When an order is placed, we handle picking, packing, shipping, and returns. FBV products get a special badge, appear higher in search results, and are eligible for same-day and next-day delivery. This service is available to Professional plan sellers.
Can I sell in multiple categories? +
Yes! You can list products across multiple categories from a single seller account. Some restricted categories (like pharmaceuticals, food supplements, electronics with safety certifications) may require additional documentation and approval. Our onboarding team will guide you through category-specific requirements during registration.
What AI tools are available for sellers? +
Professional plan sellers get access to 8 AI agents: Aria (business insights & demand forecasting), Nova (product listing optimization), Atlas (logistics & shipping optimization), Luna (customer service automation), Rex (buyer interaction assistant), Zara (pricing intelligence), Bolt (inventory management), and Echo (review & feedback analysis). These tools are available directly in your Seller Central dashboard.
