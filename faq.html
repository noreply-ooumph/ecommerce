📦 Orders & Shipping
How do I track my order? ▼

Go to the Track Order page and enter your order number (e.g. VS-2026-XXXX) along with your registered email. You'll see live courier tracking with estimated delivery time. You'll also receive SMS and email updates automatically via Zen AI.

Was this helpful?
Can I cancel or modify my order? ▼

Yes — orders can be cancelled or modified within 2 hours of placing them. After that, the order enters our fulfilment pipeline and cannot be changed. Once delivered, you can initiate a return instead. Contact us immediately via chat for the fastest resolution.

Was this helpful?
I haven't received my order — what do I do? ▼

First, check the tracking page for the latest courier status. If it shows delivered but you haven't received it, check with neighbours and building reception. If still missing, contact us within 7 days and we'll raise a courier investigation and arrange a replacement or full refund.

Was this helpful?
What are the delivery timeframes? ▼

Standard delivery: 3–5 business days (Free). Express delivery: 1–2 business days (₹99–₹149). Same-day delivery is available in select metro cities for orders placed before 12pm. Rural pincode deliveries may take 5–7 business days.

Was this helpful?
What is the free shipping threshold? ▼

Enjoy free standard shipping on all orders over ₹999. Orders below ₹999 attract a flat fee of ₹49. VibeStore Prime members get free shipping on all orders regardless of value.

Was this helpful?
What if my item arrives damaged? ▼

Take photos of the damaged item and packaging immediately, then raise a return request within 48 hours via our Returns page and select "Damaged on arrival". We'll arrange a free pickup and send a replacement or issue a full refund — your choice.

Was this helpful?
↩️ Returns & Refunds
What is your return policy duration? ▼

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy from the date of delivery. No questions asked for most products. Electronics have a 15-day return window. Certain items like innerwear, food products, and customised goods are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.

Was this helpful?
How do I initiate a return? ▼

Visit our Returns Center, select the order, choose your reason and preferred resolution. Zen AI will process your return request in under 60 seconds and schedule a free door pickup automatically.

Was this helpful?
How long does a refund take? ▼

Store credit: instant. UPI / Bank transfer: 24–48 hours. Credit/Debit card: 5–7 business days. Net banking: 3–5 business days. Refund timeline begins after we receive and inspect the returned item, which typically takes 2–3 days after pickup.

Was this helpful?
Do I need to arrange return pickup myself? ▼

No — we arrange free door-step pickup for all returns. Once your return is approved, our courier partner will contact you to schedule a convenient pickup time within 24–48 hours. Just pack the item securely in original packaging if available.

Was this helpful?
How does the exchange process work? ▼

Select "Exchange" as your preferred resolution during the return process. Specify the desired size, color, or product. We'll ship the replacement after picking up the original item. Exchange orders are prioritised and delivered within 3–4 business days of pickup.

Was this helpful?
Which items cannot be returned? ▼

Non-returnable items: innerwear / swimwear (hygiene), food & beverages, customised/personalised products, digital software licenses, items marked "Final Sale", and products with broken seals after delivery. All non-returnable items are clearly marked on their product pages.

Was this helpful?
💳 Payments
What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, BHIM), Credit & Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, Amex), Net Banking (50+ banks), BNPL (Simpl, Lazypay), No-cost EMI via major banks, Cash on Delivery, and VibeStore Wallet.

Was this helpful?
Do you offer EMI or no-cost EMI? ▼

Yes! No-cost EMI is available on orders over ₹2,999 via HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, Kotak, and IndusInd credit cards for 3, 6, and 12-month tenures. Standard EMI available on all major cards. EMI options are displayed at checkout based on your card.

Was this helpful?
My payment failed but amount was deducted — what do I do? ▼

Don't worry — the amount will automatically be refunded to your original payment method within 5–7 business days. If you don't see the refund after 7 days, contact us with your bank transaction reference and we'll resolve it immediately.

Was this helpful?
How long does my refund take to reach my account? ▼

UPI: 24–48 hours. Debit card: 3–5 days. Credit card: 5–7 days. Net Banking: 3–5 days. VibeStore Wallet: instant. These are bank processing times and may vary. You'll receive an email confirmation when we initiate the refund.

Was this helpful?
Can I get a GST invoice for my purchase? ▼

Yes! All orders automatically generate a GST invoice. Go to Account → Orders → click your order → Download GST Invoice. For B2B invoices with your company GSTIN, enter your GST number in My Account → Business Profile before placing the order.

Was this helpful?
Is my payment information secure? ▼

Yes — we use 256-bit SSL encryption, are PCI DSS compliant, and never store card details on our servers. All payments are processed via Razorpay, which meets the highest security standards. Your financial data is always safe with us.

👤 Account
How do I create an account? ▼

Click "Sign Up" on the top-right of any page. Enter your name, mobile number, and email. Verify via OTP. Your account is ready in 30 seconds! You can also sign up using Google or Apple for faster access.

Was this helpful?
I forgot my password — how do I reset it? ▼

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email or mobile number. You'll receive a password reset link or OTP. The link expires in 10 minutes. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder. You can also log in via mobile OTP without a password.

Was this helpful?
How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)? ▼

Go to Account → Settings → Security → Enable 2FA. You can use SMS OTP or an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy). 2FA adds an extra layer of security, requiring a code in addition to your password when signing in from a new device.

Was this helpful?
How do loyalty points work? ▼

Earn 1 loyalty point for every ₹10 spent. Points can be redeemed at ₹0.50 per point. Bonus points on birthdays (3x), sale events (2x), and for reviews (50 points each). Points expire after 12 months of inactivity. View your balance in Account → Loyalty Points.

Was this helpful?
How do I update my profile or address? ▼

Go to Account → Profile to update your name, phone, and email. For addresses, go to Account → Addresses. You can save up to 5 addresses and set a default for faster checkout. Changes take effect immediately.

Was this helpful?
How do I delete my account? ▼

We're sad to see you go! Go to Account → Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent and removes all order history, wishlist, and wallet balance. Outstanding refunds will be processed to your original payment method first. Contact us if you need help migrating data.

Was this helpful?

How do I know products are authentic? ▼

100% authenticity guaranteed. We source directly from brand-authorised distributors and manufacturers. Products marked "VibeStore Verified" carry our authenticity seal. Electronics come with brand warranty cards. If you ever receive a counterfeit product, we offer a full refund plus 20% compensation credit.

Was this helpful?
Where can I find the size guide? ▼

Each clothing, footwear, and accessory product page has a "Size Guide" button. It shows detailed measurements for all sizes in cm and inches. Our AI stylist can also recommend the best size based on your measurements and past purchases.

Was this helpful?
How do I get notified when a product is back in stock? ▼

On any out-of-stock product page, click "Notify Me When Available". Enter your email (or log in and it's automatic). You'll get an instant notification when stock is replenished. Manage your stock alerts in Account → Notifications → Stock Alerts.

Was this helpful?
How do I leave a product review? ▼

Only verified purchasers can leave reviews. After delivery, you'll receive a review request email. You can also go to Account → Orders → click your order → Write Review. Reviews earn 50 loyalty points. Photo reviews earn an extra 25 points. We don't remove negative reviews — all feedback is genuine.

Do you offer gift wrapping? ▼

Yes! Select "Add Gift Wrapping" at checkout for ₹49. Choose from 5 premium wrapping styles and include a personalised message card. Gift-wrapped orders come in a special VibeStore gift box. The recipient won't see the price — perfect for all occasions!

Was this helpful?
Can I place bulk or wholesale orders? ▼

Yes! For bulk orders (10+ units of the same product), email us at bulk@vibestore.in with your requirements. We offer volume discounts of 10–25% depending on quantity. Corporate gifting orders include custom branding options. We can fulfil orders up to 10,000 units with 7–10 day lead time.

How does Zen AI handle my return? ▼

When you initiate a return, Zen AI automatically verifies your eligibility, selects the fastest courier for pickup, generates a return label, schedules pickup in your preferred time slot, notifies you at each step, and triggers your refund once the item is received — all without any human intervention, in under 60 seconds.

Core AI functionality (like Zen AI for returns and Rex AI for support) is always available but you can choose to interact with humans instead by saying "Talk to human" at any time.

🏪 Sellers
How do I become a seller on VibeStore? ▼

Visit become-seller.html to register as a seller on VibeStore Marketplace. You'll need a GST number, bank account, and a valid ID. The onboarding process takes under 30 minutes and your store can go live within 24–48 hours after verification.

Was this helpful?
What are the seller fees on VibeStore Marketplace? ▼

VibeStore charges a referral fee of 2–15% depending on the product category, plus a fixed closing fee per order. There are no monthly subscription fees to list products. Detailed fee breakdowns are available in your Seller Central dashboard.

Was this helpful?
How does VibeStore Fulfilled work for sellers? ▼

With VibeStore Fulfilled, you send your inventory to our warehouses and we handle storage, packing, shipping, and customer returns on your behalf. Your products get the "VibeStore Fulfilled" badge which boosts buyer trust and increases conversions significantly.

Was this helpful?
When do sellers get paid on VibeStore? ▼

Seller payments are disbursed every 7 days to your registered bank account. The payment cycle runs from Monday to Sunday, and funds are transferred the following Wednesday. You can view all pending and completed payments in your Seller Central dashboard under Payments.

Was this helpful?
How do I handle returns from buyers as a seller? ▼

When a buyer initiates a return, you'll receive a notification in Seller Central. For VibeStore Fulfilled products, we handle the return automatically. For seller-fulfilled products, you must accept or dispute the return request within 48 hours. Approved returns are charged back to your seller account and the buyer receives their refund.

Was this helpful?
What is the seller rating system on VibeStore Marketplace? ▼

Sellers are rated on a 5-star scale based on product quality, shipping speed, and customer service. Buyers can rate and review sellers after each purchase. Sellers with ratings above 4.5 earn the "Top Seller" badge. Consistently low ratings (below 3.5) may result in listing restrictions or account review. Manage your ratings in Seller Central.
