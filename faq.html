📦 Orders
How do I track my order? ▼

Go to the Track Order page and enter your order number (e.g. VS-2024-XXXX) along with your registered email. You'll see live courier tracking with estimated delivery time. You'll also receive SMS and email updates automatically.

Can I cancel or modify my order? ▼

Yes — orders can be cancelled or modified within 2 hours of placing them. After that, the order enters our fulfilment pipeline and cannot be changed. Once delivered, you can initiate a return instead. Contact us immediately via chat for the fastest resolution.

I haven't received my order — what do I do? ▼

First, check the tracking page to see the latest courier status. If it shows delivered but you haven't received it, please check with neighbours and your building reception. If still missing, contact us within 7 days and we'll raise a courier investigation and arrange a replacement or full refund.

Can I change my delivery address after placing an order? ▼

Address changes are possible within 1 hour of ordering. After that, once the shipment is created with the courier, we are unable to redirect it. Please double-check your address before confirming checkout. Contact support immediately if you need to change it.

What if my item arrives damaged? ▼

We're sorry to hear that! Please take photos of the damaged item and packaging immediately, then raise a return request within 48 hours via our Returns page and select "Damaged on arrival". We'll arrange a free pickup and send a replacement or issue a full refund — your choice.

How do I download my invoice? ▼

Log into your Account, go to Orders, click on the order, and you'll find a "Download GST Invoice" button. Invoices are available for all completed orders. If you need a specific format for business purposes, email us and we'll send a custom invoice within 2 hours.

🚚 Shipping
What are the delivery timeframes? ▼

Standard delivery: 3–5 business days. Express delivery: 1–2 business days (additional charges apply). Same-day delivery is available in select metro cities for orders placed before 12pm. Rural pincode deliveries may take 5–7 business days.

What is the free shipping threshold? ▼

Enjoy free standard shipping on all orders over ₹999. Orders below ₹999 attract a flat shipping fee of ₹49. VibeStore Prime members get free shipping on all orders, regardless of order value.

Do you offer international shipping? ▼

Currently, we ship within India only. International shipping is on our roadmap for 2025. If you're outside India and want to place an order, you can use a freight forwarding service. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when international shipping launches.

How does express delivery work? ▼

Select "Express Delivery" at checkout. Orders placed before 2pm on business days are delivered the next business day. Orders after 2pm are delivered in 2 business days. Express is available in all major cities. A nominal fee of ₹99–₹149 applies depending on weight.

Which courier partners do you use? ▼

We partner with Delhivery, Bluedart, Ekart, and DTDC depending on your pincode and order type. Our AI routing engine automatically selects the fastest and most reliable courier for your location. Premium orders are always shipped via Bluedart.

What if my package is lost in transit? ▼

If your tracking shows no movement for 3+ business days, contact us immediately. We'll raise a courier investigation within 24 hours. If the package is confirmed lost, we'll either reship or issue a full refund — whichever you prefer. All shipments are insured.

↩️ Returns
What is your return policy duration? ▼

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy from the date of delivery. No questions asked for most products. Electronics have a 15-day return window. Certain items like innerwear, food products, and customised goods are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.

How do I initiate a return? ▼

Visit our Returns page, enter your order number and email, select the items you want to return, choose your reason and preferred resolution. Zen AI will process your return request in under 60 seconds and schedule a free pickup.

Do I need to arrange return pickup myself? ▼

No — we arrange free door-step pickup for all returns. Once your return is approved, our courier partner will contact you to schedule a convenient pickup time within 24–48 hours. You just need to pack the item securely in the original packaging if available.

How long does a refund take? ▼

Store credit: instant. UPI / Bank transfer: 24–48 hours. Credit/Debit card: 5–7 business days. Net banking: 3–5 business days. Refund timeline begins after we receive and inspect the returned item, which typically takes 2–3 days after pickup.

How does the exchange process work? ▼

Select "Exchange" as your preferred resolution during the return process. Specify the desired size, color, or product. We'll ship the replacement after picking up the original item. Exchange orders are prioritised and usually delivered within 3–4 business days of pickup.

Which items cannot be returned? ▼

Non-returnable items include: innerwear / swimwear (hygiene), food & beverages, customised/personalised products, digital software licenses, items marked "Final Sale", and products with broken seals after delivery (e.g., some health products). All non-returnable items are clearly marked on their product pages.

💳 Payments
What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept all major payment methods: UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), Credit & Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, Amex), Net Banking (50+ banks), BNPL (Buy Now Pay Later via Simpl, Lazypay), EMI (No-cost EMI via major banks), and VibeStore Wallet.

Do you offer EMI or no-cost EMI? ▼

Yes! No-cost EMI is available on orders over ₹2,999 via HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, Kotak, and IndusInd credit cards for 3, 6, and 12-month tenures. Standard EMI is available on all major cards. EMI options are displayed at checkout based on your card.

My payment failed but amount was deducted — what do I do? ▼

If your payment was deducted but the order wasn't placed, don't worry — the amount will automatically be refunded to your original payment method within 5–7 business days. If you don't see the refund after 7 days, contact us with your bank transaction reference and we'll resolve it immediately.

How long does my refund take to reach my account? ▼

Refund timelines: UPI 24–48 hours, Debit card 3–5 days, Credit card 5–7 days, Net Banking 3–5 days, VibeStore Wallet instant. These are bank processing times and may vary. You'll receive an email confirmation when we initiate the refund on our end.

Can I get a GST invoice for my purchase? ▼

Yes! All orders automatically generate a GST invoice. Go to Account → Orders → click your order → Download GST Invoice. If you need a B2B invoice with your company GSTIN, enter your GST number in My Account → Business Profile before placing the order.

How do I top up my VibeStore Wallet? ▼

Go to Account → Wallet → Add Money. You can top up using UPI, net banking, or any card. Minimum top-up is ₹100. Wallet balance never expires and earns 0.5% monthly interest on balances over ₹1,000. Wallet top-ups also get bonus cashback during promotional events.

👤 Account
How do I create an account? ▼

Click "Sign Up" on the top-right of any page. Enter your name, mobile number, and email. Verify via OTP. Your account is ready in 30 seconds! You can also sign up using Google or Apple for faster access. A VibeStore account lets you track orders, save addresses, earn loyalty points, and more.

I forgot my password — how do I reset it? ▼

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email or mobile number. You'll receive a password reset link or OTP. The link expires in 10 minutes. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder. You can also log in via mobile OTP without a password.

How do I update my delivery address? ▼

Go to Account → Addresses → Add New Address or edit an existing one. You can save up to 5 addresses (Home, Work, etc.). Mark one as default for faster checkout. You can also set different addresses for billing and shipping. Addresses can be updated anytime before an order is shipped.

How do I delete my account? ▼

We're sad to see you go! To delete your account, go to Account → Settings → Delete Account. Please note: deletion is permanent and will remove all order history, wishlist, and wallet balance. Outstanding refunds will be processed to your original payment method. Contact us if you want help migrating data first.

How do loyalty points work? ▼

Earn 1 loyalty point for every ₹10 spent. Points can be redeemed at ₹0.50 per point on any order. Bonus points on birthdays (3x), during sale events (2x), and for reviews (50 points each). Points expire after 12 months of inactivity. View your balance in Account → Loyalty Points.

How do I manage notification settings? ▼

Go to Account → Notifications to customise what you receive. You can independently toggle: order updates (recommended on), promotional emails, SMS alerts, WhatsApp messages, and push notifications. To unsubscribe from all marketing, click "Unsubscribe" at the bottom of any email.

🛍️ Products
How do I know products are authentic? ▼

100% authenticity guaranteed. We source directly from brand-authorised distributors and manufacturers. Products marked "VibeStore Verified" carry our authenticity seal. Electronics come with brand warranty cards. If you ever receive a counterfeit product, we offer a full refund plus 20% compensation credit.

Where can I find the size guide? ▼

Each clothing, footwear, and accessory product page has a "Size Guide" button. It shows detailed measurements for all sizes in cm and inches. For best fit, measure yourself and compare against the guide. Our AI stylist can also recommend the best size based on your measurements and past purchases.

How do I get notified when a product is back in stock? ▼

On any out-of-stock product page, click "Notify Me When Available". Enter your email (or log in and it's automatic). You'll get an instant notification when stock is replenished. You can manage your stock alert subscriptions in Account → Notifications → Stock Alerts.

How do I leave a product review? ▼

Only verified purchasers can leave reviews. After delivery, you'll receive a review request email. You can also go to Account → Orders → click your order → Write Review. Reviews earn 50 loyalty points. Photo reviews earn an extra 25 points. We don't remove negative reviews — all feedback is genuine.

Do you offer gift wrapping? ▼

Yes! Select "Add Gift Wrapping" at checkout for ₹49. Choose from 5 premium wrapping styles and include a personalised message card. Gift-wrapped orders come in a special VibeStore gift box. Recipient won't see the price. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, and festive occasions!

Can I place bulk / wholesale orders? ▼

Yes! For bulk orders (10+ units of same product), email us at bulk@vibestore.in with your requirements. We offer volume discounts of 10–25% depending on quantity. Corporate gifting orders include custom branding options. We can fulfil orders up to 10,000 units with 7–10 day lead time.
