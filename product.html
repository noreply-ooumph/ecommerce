Product Description

The Sony WH-1000XM5 redefines what you can expect from a premium noise-cancelling headphone. With our most advanced noise cancelling ever achieved, eight microphones and two processors work in tandem to detect the precise frequencies of noise around you and cancel them out in real time — whether you're on a packed metro, in a crowded office, or anywhere life takes you.

Experience a whole new dimension of sound quality powered by Sony's Integrated Processor V1 and the new 30mm driver units with a carbon-fibre-composite dome. Crystal clear spatial audio creates an immersive listening field that faithfully reproduces the artist's original intent, from the deepest bass to the highest treble.

Speak to be heard — but only by those you choose. Precise Voice Pickup Technology uses five microphones and advanced AI signal processing so your voice sounds natural and clear during calls, even in windy or noisy conditions. Wind noise reduction ensures your voice remains intelligible when outdoors.

Stay connected and productive without sacrificing your music. Multipoint Connection lets the WH-1000XM5 seamlessly connect to two Bluetooth devices simultaneously, so you can take calls from your phone while your laptop plays music — switching instantly without skipping a beat.

With Quick Charge technology, just 3 minutes of charging gives you 3 hours of playback. A full charge provides up to 30 hours of listening at maximum noise cancellation, or up to 40 hours with noise cancellation off. The USB-C charging port is conveniently located on the right ear cup.

The redesigned headband uses a sleek, smooth surface material that minimises pressure points on the top of your head. Ultra-soft synthetic leather cushions the ear pads, wrapping comfortably around your ears for long listening sessions. The slim, folding design makes the WH-1000XM5 easy to stow in the included premium carry case.

Questions & Answers
Does this work with Sony 360 Reality Audio?
Yes! The WH-1000XM5 fully supports Sony 360 Reality Audio when paired with the Sony Music app. You can also enjoy spatial audio from Apple Music and Amazon Music HD.

Can I use it wired when the battery dies?
Yes, you can use the included 3.5mm audio cable for wired listening. However, note that ANC and most smart features will be disabled in wired mode. The cable is a 3.5mm TRS standard cable.
