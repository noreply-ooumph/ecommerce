Parties

This Seller Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between:

  • VibeStore Marketplace Pvt Ltd, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013 with its registered office at 4th Floor, Tech Park, Sector 62, Noida, Uttar Pradesh 201301, India (hereinafter referred to as the "Platform" or "VibeStore"); and
  • The individual, sole proprietor, partnership firm, limited liability partnership, or private or public limited company registering as a seller on the Platform (hereinafter referred to as the "Seller").

This Agreement governs the terms and conditions under which the Seller may list, market, and sell products through the VibeStore Platform. By completing the seller registration process and clicking "Accept Agreement", the Seller agrees to be legally bound by this Agreement in its entirety.

Binding Agreement: This Agreement, together with the VibeStore Terms of Service, Seller Policies, and any other policies referenced herein, constitutes the complete and exclusive agreement between VibeStore and the Seller regarding the subject matter herein.

2

Seller Eligibility

To register and operate as a seller on the VibeStore Platform, you must meet the following eligibility requirements at all times:

  • Age: The Seller must be at least 18 years of age. If registering as a business entity, the authorised representative executing this Agreement must be at least 18 years of age and have legal authority to bind the business entity.
  • PAN Card: A valid Permanent Account Number (PAN) issued by the Income Tax Department of India is mandatory for all sellers, whether individuals or registered business entities. PAN is required for TDS deductions and financial reporting.
  • GST Registration: GST registration is required for sellers with an annual aggregate turnover exceeding ₹40 lakhs (₹20 lakhs for sellers in special category states). For e-commerce sellers, GST registration may be mandatory regardless of turnover — sellers are advised to consult a tax professional. The valid GSTIN must be provided and kept updated on the Platform.
  • Bank Account: The Seller must maintain a valid bank account in their name (or the registered business's name) at a scheduled commercial bank in India. This account will be used for receiving payouts from VibeStore.
  • Contact Information: A valid mobile phone number and email address are mandatory for account communications, OTP verification, and operational notifications.
  • Business Licences: Where applicable, the Seller must possess all licences, registrations, and certifications required to sell their specific product categories under applicable Indian law (e.g., FSSAI licence for food, drug licence for pharmaceuticals, ISI certification for applicable products).

Ongoing Compliance: Eligibility is an ongoing requirement. The Seller must maintain all required registrations, licences, and compliance throughout the duration of this Agreement and must promptly update VibeStore of any material changes to their eligibility status.

3

Seller Obligations

By accepting this Agreement, the Seller undertakes the following obligations:

3.1 Accurate Product Listings

  • All product descriptions, images, specifications, pricing, category classifications, and other listing information must be accurate, complete, and not misleading.
  • Product images must be original, of high quality, and accurately represent the product being sold. Stock photos and images of different products are prohibited.
  • Pricing must include all mandatory charges. Hidden fees or surprise charges at checkout are prohibited.
  • MRP, net quantity, country of origin, and all other mandatory declarations as required under the Legal Metrology (Packaged Commodities) Rules, 2011 must be prominently displayed.

3.2 Inventory and Fulfilment

  • Sellers must maintain sufficient inventory for all active listings. Listing products that are out of stock or not readily available is prohibited.
  • All confirmed orders must be shipped within the promised timeframe, with the default being 48 hours from order confirmation (excluding Sundays and public holidays).
  • Sellers must provide valid tracking information for all shipments within 24 hours of dispatch.

3.3 Buyer Communication

  • Sellers must respond to buyer queries within 24 hours of receipt. Note that Rex AI, VibeStore's AI assistant, handles a significant portion of routine buyer queries automatically.
  • All buyer communications must be professional, courteous, and accurate. Soliciting off-platform transactions or sharing contact details to avoid VibeStore's commission is strictly prohibited.

3.4 Returns and Refunds

  • Sellers must accept returns for products that are damaged, defective, or not as described, in accordance with VibeStore's Return Policy and the Consumer Protection Act, 2019.
  • Sellers must process approved refunds within the timeframes specified in VibeStore's Seller Help Centre.

3.5 Legal Compliance

  • Sellers must comply with all applicable Indian laws and regulations, including but not limited to: the Consumer Protection Act, 2019; Legal Metrology Act, 2009; Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006 (FSSAI); Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940; Information Technology Act, 2000; and the Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017.
  • Sellers must not list any prohibited items as defined in Section 6 of this Agreement or in VibeStore's Prohibited Products Policy.

3.6 Performance Standards

  • Sellers must maintain a minimum seller rating of 3.5 out of 5.0 as measured by VibeStore's seller rating system.
  • Sellers must maintain order defect rates (cancellations, returns, negative feedback) within acceptable thresholds as published in the Seller Performance Policy.

6

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account suspension and may lead to permanent termination and/or legal action:

  • Counterfeit or Replica Products: Listing, selling, or attempting to sell products that infringe upon registered trademarks or that are counterfeit, unauthorised replicas, or misrepresented as genuine branded products.
  • Price Manipulation: Artificially inflating prices before sales or promotional periods, creating false impressions of discounts, or engaging in predatory pricing that violates applicable competition law.
  • Review Manipulation: Incentivising, purchasing, or otherwise manipulating product reviews or seller ratings. This includes soliciting only positive reviews from buyers or discouraging the submission of negative reviews.
  • Off-Platform Transactions: Soliciting buyers to complete transactions outside of the VibeStore Platform to circumvent commission obligations. Sharing contact details with buyers for this purpose is also prohibited.
  • Multiple Seller Accounts: Operating more than one seller account without prior written authorisation from VibeStore. Each legal entity is permitted one seller account.
  • Misleading Listings: Using misleading product titles, descriptions, images, or attributes. This includes keyword stuffing, false claims about product features, certifications, or country of origin.
  • Prohibited Items: Listing any item falling under VibeStore's Prohibited Products Policy, including weapons, controlled substances, counterfeit goods, adult content, and stolen goods.
  • Fraudulent Activity: Any form of fraud, including false claims, identity fraud, payment fraud, or submission of false documents during seller verification.
7

Termination

7.1 Termination by Seller: The Seller may terminate this Agreement at any time by providing VibeStore with thirty (30) days' written notice via the Seller Dashboard or by email to sellerops@vibestore.in. During the notice period, the Seller must continue to fulfil all pending orders and obligations.

7.2 Termination by VibeStore: VibeStore may terminate this Agreement:

  • With 30 days' notice for any reason at its discretion;
  • Immediately and without notice in cases of material breach of this Agreement, including but not limited to: selling counterfeit goods, engaging in fraud, repeated policy violations, maintaining a seller rating below 2.5 for 30+ consecutive days, or non-compliance with applicable law;
  • Immediately upon receiving a legally valid direction from a government or regulatory authority.

7.3 Effect of Termination:

  • Upon termination, all Seller listings will be immediately deactivated and removed from the Platform.
  • The Seller must fulfil all orders that were placed prior to the termination date.
  • Outstanding payouts for orders fulfilled prior to termination will be disbursed within 30 days of the termination date, subject to any deductions for pending returns, disputes, or outstanding fees.
  • VibeStore reserves the right to withhold final payouts for a period of up to 90 days to cover potential buyer disputes or chargebacks arising from pre-termination orders.

Limitation of Liability

8.1 VibeStore's Limitation: VibeStore's liability to the Seller under this Agreement shall be limited to the total commission fees paid by the Seller to VibeStore in the three months preceding the event giving rise to the claim. VibeStore shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages, including loss of profits or business opportunities.

8.2 Platform Role: VibeStore is not liable for buyer disputes beyond facilitating the Platform's mediation process. VibeStore does not guarantee any minimum sales volumes, revenue, or business outcomes for sellers.

8.3 Seller's Full Liability: The Seller is fully and solely liable for:

  • Any product defects, safety hazards, or injuries caused by products sold through the Platform;
  • All intellectual property infringement claims arising from Seller Content or products;
  • Regulatory non-compliance, including violations of labelling laws, food safety laws, drug laws, or any other applicable regulation;
  • All tax obligations arising from sales through the Platform, including GST collection and remittance where applicable;
  • Any claims made by buyers or third parties in connection with the Seller's products or conduct.

8.4 Indemnification: The Seller agrees to defend, indemnify, and hold harmless VibeStore and its officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any and all third-party claims, liabilities, damages, judgments, losses, and expenses (including reasonable legal fees) arising from or related to: (a) Seller's breach of this Agreement; (b) Seller's products; (c) Seller's content; or (d) Seller's violation of applicable law.

9

Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. The parties agree that any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall first be subject to a 30-day informal negotiation period, after which unresolved disputes shall be referred to binding arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

The seat and venue of arbitration shall be Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India. The arbitration shall be conducted in English before a sole arbitrator mutually agreed upon by the parties. The arbitral award shall be final and binding on both parties.

For matters not subject to arbitration, the courts of competent jurisdiction in Noida, Uttar Pradesh shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

Entire Agreement: This Agreement, together with VibeStore's Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Seller Policies, and all other policies and guidelines referenced herein, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements, representations, and understandings.
