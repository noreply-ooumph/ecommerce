Common Questions

Quick answers to what most customers ask

How do I track my order?
Visit our Track Order page, enter your order number and email. You'll see real-time updates from our courier partner.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
Yes — orders can be cancelled or modified within 2 hours of placement. After that, please initiate a return once it's delivered.
How long do refunds take?
Refunds to original payment take 5–7 business days. Store credit is instant. UPI refunds arrive in 24–48 hours.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes — we use bank-grade encryption (256-bit SSL) and never store card details. All payments processed via Razorpay / Stripe.
