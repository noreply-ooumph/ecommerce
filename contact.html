Common Questions
Quick answers to what most customers ask
How do I track my order?▼

Visit our Track Order page, enter your order number and email. You'll see real-time courier updates and estimated delivery time.

Can I cancel or modify my order?▼

Yes — orders can be cancelled or modified within 2 hours of placement. After that, the order is in fulfilment. Once delivered, initiate a return instead.

How long do refunds take?▼

Store credit is instant. UPI refunds arrive in 24–48 hours. Card refunds take 5–7 business days depending on your bank.

Is my payment information secure?▼

Yes — 256-bit SSL encryption, PCI DSS compliant, and we never store card details. All payments via Razorpay/Stripe with bank-grade security.

How do I start a return?▼

Visit our Returns Center and enter your order details. Zen AI processes returns in under 60 seconds — free door pickup arranged automatically.

Do you offer EMI options?▼

Yes! No-cost EMI on orders over ₹2,999 via HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, and Kotak credit cards for 3, 6, and 12-month tenures. Select at checkout.
